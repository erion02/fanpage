Blaugrana Legends

Lionel Messi

The greatest player in Barça history. Played from 2000–2021, winning 4 Champions Leagues and 10 La Liga titles.

Xavi Hernandez

The maestro of midfield. Played 1998–2015, part of the legendary tiki-taka era and a leader on and off the pitch.

Andres Iniesta

The magician of the field. Played 2002–2018, scoring the winning goal in the 2009 Champions League final.

Gerard Pique

"The pillar of defense. Played from 2008–2023, becoming a key figure in Barça's success, winning 8 La Liga titles and 3 Champions Leagues."

Carles Puylo

"The ultimate captain and defensive wall. Played from 1999–2014, leading Barça to countless victories with his unmatched leadership and determination."

Dani Alves

"The unstoppable right-back. Played from 2008–2016 and briefly in 2021–2022, becoming the most decorated player in football history with his attacking brilliance."

Victor Valdes

"The guardian of the net. Played from 2002–2014, becoming a key part of Barça's golden era, winning 6 Zamora trophies and 3 Champions Leagues with unforgettable saves."

Ivan Rakitic

"The midfield maestro. Played from 2014–2020, scoring a vital goal in the 2015 Champions League final and contributing to Barça's domestic dominance."

Ronaldinho

"The magician with a smile. Played from 2003–2008, revolutionizing Barça with his flair, winning the 2006 Champions League and two La Liga titles."

Luis Suarez

"The lethal striker. Played from 2014–2020, becoming one of Barça’s greatest goalscorers with his relentless finishing and incredible partnership in attack."

Neymar Jr

"The Brazilian sensation. Played from 2013–2017, dazzling fans with his skill and flair, forming part of the iconic MSN trio and winning the 2015 Champions League."

Sergio Busquets

"The silent genius. Played from 2008–2023, mastering the art of midfield control and becoming the backbone of Barça’s tiki-taka dominance."
